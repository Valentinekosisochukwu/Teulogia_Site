Teulogia is a language software that
recognizes Sign Language and translates it
into the spoken word, and vice versa using
Machine Learning. All you need is a
smartphone or laptop to open up the world
of communication to the Deaf and Hard of
Hearing.

Teulogia Web


Provide an accessible experience to Deaf and Hard of Hearing individuals by translating your website into ASL. When a user selects text on your website with this extension enabled, a signing avatar will pop up and sign the text in ASL.
